About

Bridge Cottage is a luxuriously appointed property set in delightful rural surroundings amidst open countryside at Starnthwaite Ghyll, half a mile north of Crosthwaite village at the head of the Lyth Valley. Kendal and Windermere are both five miles away. The River Gilpin tumbles over a waterfall and then flows underneath Bridge Cottage as a unique feature.
